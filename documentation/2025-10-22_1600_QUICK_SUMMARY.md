# 🎉 Quick Summary - Archive, Delete & Notifications

## ✅ What's New (October 22, 2025 - 16:00)

### 1. 📦 Archive Conversations
- **Where:** Three-dot menu (⋮) next to direct messages
- **What:** Hide conversations from your list
- **Who:** Both admin and EOD users

### 2. 🗑️ Delete Group Chats
- **Where:** Three-dot menu (⋮) next to group chats
- **What:** Permanently delete group chats (with confirmation)
- **Who:** Both admin and EOD users

### 3. 🔴 Unread Message Badges
- **Admin:** Red badge on "Messages" in Sidebar
- **EOD User:** Red badge on "Messages" tab in EOD Portal
- **What:** Shows total unread message count
- **Updates:** Real-time (instant)

---

## 🎯 How to Use

### Archive a Conversation:
1. Go to Messages
2. Find a direct conversation
3. Click **⋮** (three dots)
4. Click **"Archive"**
5. Done! Conversation is hidden

### Delete a Group Chat:
1. Go to Messages
2. Find a group chat (blue highlight)
3. Click **⋮** (three dots)
4. Click **"Delete Group"** (red text)
5. Confirm deletion
6. Done! Group is deleted

### Check Unread Messages:
- **Admin:** Look at Sidebar → "Messages" link
- **EOD User:** Look at EOD Portal → "Messages" tab
- **Red badge shows count** (e.g., "3" means 3 unread messages)

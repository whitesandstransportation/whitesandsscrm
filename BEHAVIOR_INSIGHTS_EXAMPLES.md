# Deep Behavior Trend Detection - Example Insights

This feature analyzes user work patterns and generates warm, supportive behavioral insights. Here are examples of the insights users will see:

## 🌟 Energy Patterns

**Your mornings show strong focus — a great time for deep or creative work.**
> Consider protecting these early hours for your most important tasks.

**Your momentum builds as the day progresses — you're a strong finisher.**
> Use mornings for planning and warming up. Save execution for when you hit your stride.

**You start strong and love morning momentum — that's your superpower.**
> Front-load important decisions and creative work when your energy is highest.

## ⏰ Timing Patterns

**Your flow strengthens around mid-morning — great time to protect for important work.**
> This is your natural power window. Use it wisely for complex projects.

**Your early afternoon pace is steady — ideal for structured or repetitive tasks.**
> This consistent energy is perfect for tasks that need focus but not peak creativity.

**Evenings stay calm and consistent — wonderful for wrapping up loose ends.**
> This is a great time for review work, planning, or lighter administrative tasks.

## 🎯 Focus Patterns

**Mid-week brings your longest focus streaks — a perfect spot for complex projects.**
> Save your challenging work for Tuesday and Wednesday when your rhythm is strongest.

**You create beautiful flow states with multiple tasks in a row — that's rare and valuable.**
> Protect these flow windows from interruptions. They're where your best work happens.

**You maintain strong focus during tasks — that's a valuable natural strength.**
> Remember to take intentional breaks between tasks to stay fresh and energized.

## 💖 Balance & Wellness

**Pauses tend to increase after 2 PM — that's completely natural.**
> Try keeping afternoon tasks light and flexible. Your body knows what it needs.

**Fridays show a gentle dip in focus — consider lighter tasks for a balanced end to your week.**
> Honor your natural rhythm. Fridays are perfect for wrapping up, not diving deep.

**You balance variety beautifully — working across different projects keeps your mind fresh.**
> This natural variety is healthy. Keep mixing tasks to maintain engagement.

**You can sustain long focus sessions — that's impressive dedication.**
> Consider gentle micro-breaks every 60-90 minutes. Your future self will thank you.

## 🏆 Strength Patterns

**You naturally build momentum by Monday — that's a wonderful way to start the week.**
> Keep riding this wave. Your Monday energy sets a positive tone for the entire week.

**You complete tasks fastest before noon — amazing momentum to start your day.**
> Batch similar tasks during these hours for maximum efficiency and satisfaction.

**Your task completion pace is wonderfully steady — a sign of healthy work rhythm.**
> This consistency is a real strength. Trust your natural timing on new tasks.

**You bounce back quickly after breaks — a strong sign of healthy task rhythm.**
> Your ability to refocus is excellent. Keep honoring both work and rest.

**You finish what you start — that's a powerful habit that builds momentum.**
> Your follow-through is excellent. Trust yourself with bigger challenges.

## 🌱 Early Stage (Less than 5 tasks)

**You're building your work rhythm — keep tracking to unlock personalized insights.**
> Each task you complete helps us understand your natural flow better.

## Key Features

✅ **Always positive and encouraging** - Never judgmental or critical
✅ **Pattern + Suggestion format** - Observable behavior followed by gentle recommendation
✅ **Category-based color coding** - Visual distinction between insight types
✅ **Animated cards** - Smooth fade-in with staggered timing
✅ **Real-time analysis** - Based on last 7 days of actual work data
✅ **Research-backed** - Patterns aligned with productivity science

## Technical Implementation

- **Analysis Period**: Past 7 days of time entries
- **Minimum Data**: 5 tasks required for meaningful patterns
- **Update Frequency**: Real-time with Supabase subscriptions
- **Categories**: Energy, Timing, Focus, Balance, Strength, Wellness
- **Display**: 8-12 most relevant insights per user
- **Tone**: Human, warm, supportive, non-judgmental

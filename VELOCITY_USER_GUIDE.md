# 🚀 Understanding Your Velocity Score — User Guide

## What is Velocity?

**Velocity** measures how much valuable work you complete per hour. It's not just about finishing tasks quickly — it's about completing **high-value, complex work** efficiently.

Think of it as your **productivity output rate** that rewards both **quality** and **speed**.

---

## 🎯 How It Works (Simple Version)

Your Velocity score is based on:

1. **What type of tasks you complete** (Quick, Standard, Deep Work, Long, Very Long)
2. **How important those tasks are** (Immediate Impact, Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Evergreen)
3. **How long you spend on them** (active work time, not pauses)
4. **Special bonuses** for sustained deep work and complex task days

---

## 📊 What Each Task Type Earns

### **Base Points by Task Type:**

| Task Type | Points | What It Means |
|-----------|--------|---------------|
| **Quick Task** | 1 point | Fast, simple tasks (emails, quick updates) |
| **Standard Task** | 1 point | Regular tasks (meetings, routine work) |
| **Deep Work Task** | 3 points | Complex, focused work (coding, writing, analysis) |
| **Long Task** | 4 points | Extended, challenging work (projects, reports) |
| **Very Long Task** | 5 points | Major projects (strategic planning, big deliverables) |

**Key Insight:** Deep Work and Long tasks earn **3-5× more points** than Quick tasks because they create more value!

---

## ⚡ Priority Multipliers

Your task's priority affects its value:

| Priority | Multiplier | Impact |
|----------|------------|--------|
| **Immediate Impact** | 1.4× | +40% boost (urgent, high-value work) |
| **Daily Task** | 1.2× | +20% boost (consistent daily work) |
| **Weekly Task** | 1.0× | Baseline (standard value) |
| **Trigger Task** | 1.0× | Baseline (reactive work) |
| **Monthly Task** | 0.9× | -10% (strategic, less urgent) |
| **Evergreen Task** | 0.8× | -20% (long-term, flexible timing) |

**Key Insight:** Even Evergreen tasks now contribute **meaningfully** to your velocity. We want you to work on long-term projects!

---

## 🎁 Special Bonuses

### **1. Long Session Bonus (90+ Minutes)**

If you work on a task for **90+ minutes**, you get extra points:
- **+0.3 points** for every extra 30 minutes beyond 90 minutes
- Example: A 150-minute task gets **+0.6 bonus points**

**Why?** Sustained deep work sessions deserve extra credit!

---

### **2. Deep Work Day Bonus**

If **60% or more** of your completed tasks are Deep Work, Long, or Very Long:
- **+15% velocity boost** for the day

If **50% or more** are Long or Very Long tasks:
- **+10% velocity boost** for the day

**Why?** We don't want you to feel "unproductive" just because you spent the day on complex work!

## 🧮 Example Calculations

### **Example 1: Quick Task Day**

You complete **10 Quick tasks** in **2 hours**:
```
10 tasks × 1 point = 10 points
10 points ÷ 2 hours = 5 points/hour
Velocity: (5 ÷ 5) × 100 = 100% 🎉
```

**Result:** Excellent velocity! Quick wins build momentum.

---

### **Example 2: Deep Work Day**

You complete **2 Deep Work tasks (Immediate Impact)** in **4 hours**:
```
Task 1: 3 pts × 1.4× = 4.2 points
Task 2: 3 pts × 1.4× = 4.2 points
Total: 8.4 points ÷ 4 hours = 2.1 points/hour
Deep Work Bonus: 2.1 × 1.15 = 2.415 points/hour (100% deep work)
Velocity: (2.415 ÷ 5) × 100 = 48% ✅
```

**Result:** Strong velocity for strategic work! You're creating high value.

---

### **Example 3: Long Session with Bonus**

You complete **1 Deep Work task (Daily)** that runs **150 minutes**:
```
Base: 3 pts × 1.2× = 3.6 points
Long Session Bonus: (150 - 90) ÷ 30 = 2 blocks × 0.3 = +0.6 points
Total: 4.2 points ÷ 2.5 hours = 1.68 points/hour
Velocity: (1.68 ÷ 5) × 100 = 34% ✅
```

**Result:** Good velocity for sustained focus! Your long session got extra credit.

---

### **Example 4: Balanced Day**

You complete **3 Quick tasks** and **2 Deep Work tasks** in **4 hours**:
```
Quick: 3 × 1 = 3 points
Deep Work: 2 × 3 = 6 points
Total: 9 points ÷ 4 hours = 2.25 points/hour
Velocity: (2.25 ÷ 5) × 100 = 45% ✅
```

**Result:** Excellent balance! Quick wins + deep work = optimal productivity.

## 🎯 Common Questions

### **Q: Why is my velocity lower on deep work days?**
**A:** Deep Work tasks take longer, so velocity naturally drops. BUT the system now gives you:
- Higher base points (3 pts instead of 1 pt)
- Long session bonuses (+0.3 pts per 30 min over 90 min)
- Deep work day bonus (+15% if 60%+ of tasks are deep/long)

So your velocity is **much fairer** now!

---

### **Q: Should I only do Quick tasks to maximize velocity?**
**A:** No! While Quick tasks can give you 100% velocity, they create less business value. The best approach is:
- **Mix quick wins with deep work**
- **Focus on high-priority tasks** (Immediate Impact, Daily)
- **Embrace sustained focus sessions**

The system now rewards **strategic balance**, not just speed.

---

### **Q: Do Evergreen tasks hurt my velocity?**
**A:** Not anymore! Evergreen tasks now have a **0.8× multiplier** (improved from 0.6×). Plus:
- If you complete a Very Long Evergreen task, you get **5 pts × 0.8× = 4 points**
- Long sessions get bonus points
- The system recognizes strategic work in insights

You're **encouraged** to work on long-term projects!

---

### **Q: What if I pause a lot?**
**A:** Pauses don't count toward your active time, so they don't directly hurt velocity. However:
- Pauses slow down task completion
- Slower completion = lower velocity
- Try to minimize interruptions for best results

---

### **Q: How does velocity relate to other metrics?**
**A:** Velocity is **one piece** of the productivity puzzle:
- **Efficiency** = How well you use your clocked-in time
- **Completion** = How well you finish high-priority tasks and meet estimates
- **Focus** = How well you maintain concentration
- **Velocity** = How much valuable work you complete per hour

All metrics together paint the **full picture** of your productivity!
